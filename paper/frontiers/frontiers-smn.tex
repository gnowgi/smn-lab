\title[The Sensation Modulating Network]{The Sensation Modulating Network: an opponent architecture for embodied cognition, with haltability as the ground of object-directedness}

\author[\firstAuthorLast ]{\Authors}
\address{}
\correspondance{}
\extraAuth{}

\begin{abstract}
\section{}
Cognitive science is divided between cognitivism, which explains the generativity of thought but detaches it from the body, and 4E approaches, which embody cognition but under-specify its mechanism and rarely commit to falsifiable predictions. We propose the \textbf{Sensation Modulating Network (SMN)}, an architecture in which cognition is the modulation of sensation through action. Its elementary unit is the \emph{Sensation Modulator}, a tissue complex that senses and acts through one substrate; opponent pairs of these form \emph{Coordinated Action Zones} that hold a dynamic equilibrium against gravity and the medium. We give the single-zone dynamics as a closed formal system, including a \emph{haltability} operator whose state variable --- \emph{alert energy} --- is the real metabolic cost of holding an antagonistic configuration. We argue that haltability is the architectural ground of \emph{object-directedness}: an object is registered as \emph{other} when an unpredicted resistance halts an ongoing action. Reafference separates self from world; a broadcast operator coordinates a decentralized network; and layered action patterns --- basal, haltable, and negotiable --- connect the grounded body to the recombinable structure the cognitivist tradition prizes, the last stated as a hypothesis whose demonstration we defer to a companion paper. The architecture yields eight measurable \emph{registers} (architectural fingerprints), and we report a confirm-and-correct ledger from an open simulation bench, SMN-Lab, that tests them. We position the SMN by its affinities and differences with active inference, Global Workspace Theory, G\"ardenfors's conceptual spaces, and Levin's basal cognition, and we name the wet-lab measurement that would decide its central energetic claim.

\section{Introduction}\label{sec:intro}

Two traditions divide the science of mind. \textbf{Cognitivism} explains the generativity of thought --- its recursive, compositional productivity --- but treats the body as peripheral input and output to a central computation. \textbf{4E cognition} (embodied, embedded, enactive, extended) returns thought to the body and its world, but has been weaker on the nervous system's specific organization and, more consequentially for a science, weaker on \emph{falsifiable mechanism}. Neither tradition has produced a unified account that explains both the grounded character of animal cognition and the generative character of human thought, and that commits to predictions it could fail.

This paper offers such an account and states it so that it can be tested. The proposal is the \textbf{Sensation Modulating Network (SMN)}: an architecture in which cognition is the \emph{modulation of sensation through action}. The architecture is built from one repeated element --- a tissue complex that senses and acts through a single substrate --- organized by opponency into a body-spanning network that holds itself in dynamic equilibrium against the fields it lives in. We summarize the architecture compactly, give its single-zone dynamics as a closed formal system, and derive measurable signatures from it; we then report computational tests and locate the account among its neighbours.

The organizing thesis concerns \textbf{haltability}. Classical accounts of action emphasize initiation; the SMN emphasizes the active \emph{hold}. A body built from opponent pairs can recruit a co-active equilibrium in which two pulls cancel and a configuration is held still --- not by relaxing and not by inhibiting one side, but by spending energy to maintain a balance. We call this capacity haltability and its state variable \emph{alert energy}, a non-negative, dimensionful quantity that measures the metabolic cost of the hold. The central claim is that haltability is the architectural ground of \emph{object-directedness}: when an ongoing action meets a resistance it did not predict, the same antagonistic substrate that can hold a posture halts the action, and in that halt a portion of the world is registered, for the first time, as other than the self that was acting. Aboutness does not require a symbol pointing at an object; it requires a body whose action can be stopped by what it is about.

The paper makes three contributions. First (Sections~\ref{sec:commitments}--\ref{sec:network}), it specifies the architecture and brings its formalism into the open: the Sensation Modulator, the Coordinated Action Zone with its haltability operator, reafference, the broadcast network, and the layered action patterns. Second (Section~\ref{sec:registers}), it derives eight \emph{registers} --- concrete, measurable behavioural signatures that the architecture either exhibits or does not. Third (Sections~\ref{sec:affinities}--\ref{sec:discussion}), it positions the account by its affinities and differences with neighbouring frameworks and reports a confirm-and-correct ledger from an open bench (SMN-Lab) that puts the registers at risk.

A note on scope. The architecture reaches from basal autonomic patterns up to the negotiable, recombinable patterns we argue ground generative structure; but the full account of that last step --- how negotiable action patterns underwrite generative grammar --- is the subject of a companion paper \citep{NagarjunaKarnamNAPprep} and is here stated only as a hypothesis (Section~\ref{sec:network}). The architecture brackets phenomenology throughout: it specifies the structural conditions object-directedness requires, not the felt character that may occupy them.

\section{The architecture by commitments}\label{sec:commitments}

The SMN rests on six commitments, of two kinds: empirical generalizations the architecture takes on, and structural or design commitments about its elements.

\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{C1 (opponency at every scale).} The body is organized by opponent dynamics at every scale at which it is organized --- biochemical, autonomic, motor, bilateral/segmental, neural, perceptual. The architectural primitive at every scale is an opponent pair whose dynamics produce a balance state. This is an empirical observation, not a modeling convenience.
\item \textbf{C2 (Sensation Modulators everywhere).} The smallest element is the Sensation Modulator (\sm): a single tissue complex that both senses and acts through one substrate, with a mechanical interface engaging body geometry and a messaging interface engaging the network. The architecture is neutral about what carries the messages but commits to the structural fact that sensing and acting share a substrate.
\item \textbf{C3 (decentralized coordination through broadcast).} Each \caz{} runs its own balance computation locally, from its own afferents and the broadcast it reads from the rest of the body. Coordination is a property of the network, not a function any centre computes.
\item \textbf{C4 (active-perception filtering).} Each board admits to its predictive computation only sensor streams currently coupled to its modulation; uncoupled streams remain under low-cost peripheral monitoring. With a body producing far more data than local computation can attend to, the architecture filters at ingress \citep{OReganNoe2001Sensorimotor}.
\item \textbf{C5 (habituation).} Streams that are coupled but fully predicted by an ongoing basal pattern --- heartbeat, breathing, postural sway --- drop out of attended status because their coupling carries no residual information.
\item \textbf{C6 (spatial cognition through differential displacement).} Spatial structure is constructible only from the differential displacement the modulators produce, registered against the axes the body's developmental asymmetries establish. The SMN is fundamentally geometric: what it constructs is a body-anchored spatial model of itself and the world.
\end{itemize}

C1 and C6 are empirical generalizations that sustained contrary evidence would falsify; C2 and C3 fix the elementary units and their coherence; C4 and C5 are parsimony commitments made at instantiation. The architecture is composed by repetition: opponent pairs of Sensation Modulators form Coordinated Action Zones, and a broadcast integrates many zones into one network (Figure~\ref{fig:architecture}). Control is \emph{morphological computation} \citep{PfeiferBongard2006}: the coupled body performs work a central controller would otherwise do.

\section{The Sensation Modulator and the Coordinated Action Zone}\label{sec:caz}

\subsection{The Sensation Modulator}
The Sensation Modulator is specified by two coupled relations:
\begin{align}
F &= \Phi(a,\,q,\,\dot q), \qquad F\geq 0 \;\text{(pull-only)} \label{eq:Phi}\\
s &= \Psi(q,\,\dot q,\,F). \label{eq:Psi}
\end{align}
Equation~\eqref{eq:Phi} states that the force the element produces depends jointly on the activation it receives and its configuration and velocity, with the pull-only constraint reflecting the biological fact that muscle and analogous tissues pull but do not push. Equation~\eqref{eq:Psi} states that the afferent the element emits is read out by receptors embedded in the \emph{same} tissue whose deformation produces $F$ --- so the same $q$ that sets $F$ via $\Phi$ also sets $s$ via $\Psi$. There is no internal boundary between ``the sensor'' and ``the actuator''; this is the \emph{dual interface}, supported by the co-placement of muscle spindles, Golgi tendon organs, and joint receptors \citep{ProskeGandevia2012}. The commitments are about the \emph{structure} of $\Phi$ and $\Psi$, not their parametric form: any $(\Phi,\Psi)$ that is pull-only and reports a readable configuration variable is an admissible realization (vertebrate or invertebrate muscle, or artificial actuators such as pneumatic or dielectric-elastomer ``muscles''), and the architecture's downstream claims survive substitution.

\subsection{The Coordinated Action Zone}
A Coordinated Action Zone (\caz) is the tuple $(Z_+, Z_-, B, H)$: two opponent modulators, a communication board $B$, and a haltability operator $H$, sharing a configuration $(q,\dot q)$ and an internal alert energy $E_R$. For a one-degree-of-freedom joint with moment arm $r$ and inertia $I$,
\begin{equation}
I\,\ddot q = r\,(F_+ - F_-) - b\,\dot q, \label{eq:joint}
\end{equation}
with $F_\pm = \Phi(a_\pm,\ell_\pm,\dot\ell_\pm)$ and $\ell_\pm = \ell_0 \mp r q$. What is non-standard is that $a_+$ and $a_-$ are not independent commands from a controller: they are emitted by the board. The agent is placed in a gravitational field treated as a \emph{structural prior} --- an ambient ordering the body did not choose --- and the opponent pair is organized to oppose it, holding a \emph{dynamic equilibrium} that action departs from rather than a setpoint a controller tracks.

\subsection{The communication board}
The board does three things: it \emph{reads} the afferent each zone emits, it \emph{routes} that afferent to the partner zone (the inverse coupling that makes the two zones behave like the pans of a balance), and it \emph{applies modulation} --- a task drive, a halt gate, and an alert-energy bias. With $g\in\{0,1\}$ the halt gate ($g=1$ active, $g=0$ halted), $\lambda$ the task equilibrium, and $a_0>0$ the baseline co-contraction,
\begin{equation}
g=0:\;\; a_+=a_-=a_0; \qquad
g=1:\;\; \text{drive}=K_p(\lambda-q)-K_d\dot q, \label{eq:drive}
\end{equation}
with the active and partner activations set by the drive sign and biased by $E_R$ through coefficients $(\beta,\gamma)$ (full routing in the Appendix). The board is not a message bus carrying addressed packets, and the broadcast it reads from is not a non-physical signal from a centre. The broadcast is \emph{physically realized but functionally individuated and medium-independent}: every message is a physical state --- a tension, a rate, a potential --- in a shared medium that any zone writes to and reads from by changing a shared variable (a hormonal milieu, a bioelectric field, global postural tension). A signal here is just a difference in one part of the body that makes a difference to another; nothing contentful is shipped and nothing reads it from outside.

\subsection{Haltability and alert energy}\label{sec:halt}
Haltability is the capacity to actively hold a configuration still, distinct from passive relaxation and from classical inhibition. It is a property of the antagonism between the two zones, not something the board generates: two pull-only zones whose forces oppose along a shared variable \emph{admit} a co-active equilibrium in which the forces cancel and the configuration is held without motion --- as the coupling of a constrained mass makes a pendulum's stable attractor exist. The board does not produce that state; it \emph{recruits} the affordance and maintains it. The state variable of the recruitment is the \emph{alert energy} $E_R\geq 0$:
\begin{equation}
\frac{dE_R}{dt} = \rho\,F_{\text{active}}\,\mathbf{1}[g=1] - \frac{E_R}{\tau_E}, \label{eq:alertenergy}
\end{equation}
where $F_{\text{active}}=\max(F_+,F_-)$, $\rho$ is the build rate, and $\tau_E$ the decay constant. Alert energy accumulates while a zone is driven, in proportion to its load, and decays passively otherwise; its loaded steady state is
\begin{equation}
E_R^* = \rho\,\tau_E\,\langle F_{\text{active}}\rangle. \label{eq:Estar}
\end{equation}
Three consequences follow. \emph{Alert energy is a real metabolic quantity}, not a free parameter: bounded, dimensionful, passively decaying, and predicted to be measurable as the metabolic difference between an engaged-but-not-active zone and the same zone at rest. \emph{Active equilibrium} (the inactive zone tonically engaged in proportion to the active zone's load) is the loaded steady state of \eqref{eq:alertenergy}. And the \emph{distinction from classical inhibition is sharp}: classical inhibition has no alert energy --- the inactive zone is silenced, with no recruited affordance to maintain. The dynamics of $E_R$ are what the SMN claims biological tissue does and reciprocal inhibition does not, and they yield the load-coupling and post-release signatures of Section~\ref{sec:registers}.

\section{Transducer, reafference, and directedness}\label{sec:reafference}

\subsection{The transducer and the reafference principle}
Unlike the Sensation Modulator, a \emph{transducer} is read-only: it maps a physical field to a signal, $S:\Phi(t,x)\mapsto s(t)$, without writing back to the body. For an agent with configuration $\theta$ and a stimulus at $\varphi$, a Gaussian receptive field gives
\begin{equation}
s(t) = I\exp\!\Big(-\tfrac{(\theta(t)-\varphi(t))^2}{\sigma^2}\Big) + \eta(t), \label{eq:rf}
\end{equation}
with $s$ depending jointly on $\theta$ (the agent's configuration, modulable by action) and $\varphi$ (the world, only conditionally observable). A transducer can register that something is happening; it cannot, alone, say whether the agent or the world caused it. The SMN resolves this with the \emph{reafference principle} \citep{vonHolstMittelstaedt1950,CrapseSommer2008}: a predictor in the board uses the directly observed $\theta$ and the agent's world estimate $\hat\varphi$ to predict $\hat s = I\exp(-(\theta-\hat\varphi)^2/\sigma^2)$ and forms the \emph{reafference residual}
\begin{equation}
r(t) = s(t) - \hat s(t). \label{eq:residual}
\end{equation}
The residual is small for self-generated change (the predictor tracks) and large for world-generated change (the estimate is outdated); it is the architecture's primitive signal of \emph{self versus world}. When the world estimate adapts by a gradient step $\dot{\hat\varphi} = \kappa\,r/(\partial\hat s/\partial\varphi)$, the post-change residual decays --- and that decay \emph{is} mastery of a sensorimotor contingency in O'Regan and No\"e's sense \citep{OReganNoe2001Sensorimotor}, now a measurable adaptation rather than a stipulated competence.

\subsection{Surprise as attention; halt as directedness}
The residual also feeds alert energy, $dE_R/dt = \rho F_{\text{active}}\mathbf 1[g{=}1] + \rho_r|r| - E_R/\tau_E$, and an unpredicted event auto-halts the drive when $|r|>\tau_h$. The \emph{same} alert-energy substrate that distinguishes haltability from inhibition is thus the substrate of attention. This yields an interruption story --- surprise gates action --- but the thesis claims more: that haltability is the condition object-directedness requires. The recruited halt is not stillness; it is a configuration \emph{held available} (a stance toward a state, not a generator of one), \emph{held interruptibly} (releasable by the world, unlike a thermostat, which cannot be surprised), and \emph{held under prediction} (the residual reports that \emph{this} thing is deviating from expectation). Together these are \emph{architectural directedness} --- the structural form of being directed at something. This is the architectural form of what Smith calls \emph{registration}: objecthood is an achievement of engaged participation, not a category the world supplies and the mind detects \citep{Smith1996OriginObjects}.

\subsection{An embodied two-vector model}
The account has a close affinity with G\"ardenfors's analysis of events in conceptual spaces \citep{Gardenfors2020Events,Gardenfors2019Robot}. An SMN agent pressing on the world is an embodied realization of the two-vector model: the antagonist pull is the \emph{force vector}, the object's response (it yields, resists, or complies) is the \emph{result vector}, and gravity and friction enter as counterforces. Objecthood, for this agent, simply \emph{is} the force-to-result mapping, sensed as resistance to its own modulation. The one refinement we add is that the agent does not \emph{learn} the mapping probabilistically: it \emph{enacts} it by construction, through the opposition of its pulls against the field. The force is physically real; what is constructed is the objecthood the agent reads off it.

\subsection{What sets the focus, and the status of the world estimate}
Two clarifications. First, haltability \emph{resolves} a focus but does not \emph{originate} the caring that makes a focus worth having; what sets which configuration is worth holding comes from the organism's biological normativity (what matters to a self-maintaining body) and its sedimented experiential history, not from the halt mechanism itself. Second, the world estimate $\hat\varphi$ is meant deflationary: for the living agent there is no stored, contentful world-model, only a current attunement constituted in coupling. ``Model'' in the representational sense is reserved for the artificial case, where structure genuinely is stored and positional --- an asymmetry between natural and artificial cognition we develop elsewhere.

\section{Networks, broadcast, and the layered action patterns}\label{sec:network}

\subsection{The dual-signal property}
A multi-zone body runs coordinated activation trajectories. Write $y_t = F(\boldsymbol\theta_t; z_t)$ for the body's own modulation map from its joint configuration $\boldsymbol\theta_t$ to its afferent vector, with $z_t$ the fixed background. A trajectory is \emph{dual-signal} when two zones $Z_x,Z_y$ are sensitive to a common parameter $\theta$ (DS-1), both efference copies are available to the predictor through the broadcast (DS-2), and the pattern is agent-haltable (DS-3). The dual-signal property makes self-contact, world-contact, and contact with another agent into three \emph{structurally distinct} residual signatures, distinguishable by the form of the residual rather than its magnitude --- the architectural basis of the self/world/other distinction (registers 5 and 8, Section~\ref{sec:registers}).

\subsection{The phase-oscillator picture and four regimes}
A chain of $N$ segmental zones is described by Kuramoto-style coupled oscillators with modulatory input \citep{Kuramoto1984Oscillations}:
\begin{equation}
\dot\theta_i = \omega_i + \sum_{j\in\mathcal N(i)} K_{ij}\sin(\theta_j-\theta_i-\varphi_{ij}) + u_i(t), \label{eq:kuramoto}
\end{equation}
where $u_i$ is not stipulated but computed by the $i$th zone's board and haltability operator. Four regimes follow: a \textbf{Basal Action Pattern} (\bap; $u_i\equiv0$, a stable limit cycle --- heartbeat, gait, breathing); a \textbf{Haltable Action Pattern} (\hap; $u_i$ a halt pulse, with $E_R$ governed by \eqref{eq:alertenergy}); a \textbf{Negotiable Action Pattern} (\nap; $u_i$ continuously rephases or reweights the coupling in response to perceptual predicates); and a \textbf{Transactional Action Pattern} (\tap; a \nap{} whose halt events are externalized as a public trace recoverable by other agents).

\subsection{The network and the broadcast}
The full network is the tuple $\mathrm{SMN}=(G,\mathcal Z,\mathcal B,\mathcal H,\mathcal S,\Pi,\alpha)$: a routing graph $G$ over zone nodes $\mathcal Z$, the local boards $\mathcal B$ and haltability operators $\mathcal H$, the sensor space $\mathcal S$, the \emph{broadcast operator} $\Pi$ that makes one zone's state available to another's predictor, and the active-perception filter $\alpha$ of C4. The broadcast is the decentralized substitute for a central controller (C3): it maintains a shared state-space that every zone contributes to and reads from. The single-zone dynamics above are specified as a closed system; the analytical theory of multi-zone networks --- stability, controllability, convergence --- is, by our own statement, deferred, and the computational bench of Section~\ref{sec:ledger} is where the network-level claims are exercised in the interim.

\subsection{From negotiable patterns to generative structure (hypothesis)}
The basal and haltable patterns above are fully within the present account. The negotiable patterns point beyond it. \nap{}s are modifiable, recombinable primitives: a zone or sub-network can rephase, regate, and re-route on the fly, and nested zones can be recruited and released as units. We hypothesize that this recombinability --- \caz{} switching over a nested architecture, with externalized traces (\tap{}s) crossing body boundaries --- is where the recursive, generative structure the cognitivist tradition locates in grammar \citep{chomsky1957syntactic} becomes architecturally tractable: generativity grounded in body-anchored modular dynamics rather than in disembodied symbols. We state this as a hypothesis and defer its demonstration to a companion paper \citep{NagarjunaKarnamNAPprep}; the present paper establishes the \bap{} and \hap{} layers it would build on.

\section{The eight predicted registers}\label{sec:registers}

The architecture commits to eight \emph{registers} --- measurable signatures, each derived from the formalism, that a real or simulated system either exhibits or does not. They are the architecture's falsifiers.

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Tonic load coupling.} The inactive zone's tonic activation rises linearly with the active zone's load, $a_{\text{partner}}\approx a_0+\beta\rho\tau_E F_{\text{active}}$, where classical inhibition predicts independence of load.
\item \textbf{Resumption latency.} After a brief halt, latency to a small reversed target is shorter than under classical inhibition by $\Delta t\approx \gamma E_R(t_{\text{release}})/K_p$.
\item \textbf{Reafference (self vs world).} For a fixed sensor change the residual is small when self-generated and large when world-generated; in the noiseless limit the self-residual is zero.
\item \textbf{Sensorimotor-contingency mastery.} With world-model adaptation on, the post-change residual decays to the noise floor; the decay rate is mastery, made measurable.
\item \textbf{Intra-body dual-signal residual.} Regressing the residual on the partner-as-stimulus form gives $R^2\to1$ for two zones of one SMN sharing a substrate, and $R^2\to0$ for an exogenous stimulus --- a structural, not magnitude, signature.
\item \textbf{Surprise-driven attention and auto-halt.} An unpredicted event drives the residual above threshold and gates the drive within one step; attention and haltability share one substrate.
\item \textbf{Shared intentionality (hypothesis).} Two agents in mutual reafference, both adapting, show a joint residual that decays far faster than under asymmetric or no adaptation \citep{Tomasello2005Understanding}.
\item \textbf{Self / world / other.} The three dual-signal indices give a three-way structural distinction between broadcast-internal contact, contact with a non-intentional exterior, and contact with another agent's structured modulation.
\end{enumerate}

Three further consequences of the network formalism (basal-pattern entrainment of haltable zones, axial-to-appendicular mode-switching, Hebbian recruitment of negotiable patterns) presuppose a multi-zone implementation and are flagged as targets rather than numbered registers.

\section{Affinities and differences}\label{sec:affinities}

The SMN shares commitments with several well-developed programs, and locating it among them is best done by naming what it shares and where it diverges, not by displacing anything.

\paragraph{Active inference and predictive processing.} The relationship is more reconcilable than an oppositional reading suggests \citep{Friston2010FreeEnergyPrinciple,ParrPezzuloFriston2022}. The mathematics is largely shared: the reafference residual $r=s-\hat s$ is structurally a prediction error, equation~\eqref{eq:alertenergy} couples surprise to attention and auto-halt, and registers 3 and 6 are contrasts active inference also predicts. Both accounts are hierarchical, and both make the body act as a unit --- active inference through a global free-energy quantity, the SMN through the broadcast operator $\Pi$ that integrates local zones into a shared state-space. Indeed the activated subset of the network (the zones currently coupled through haltability and broadcast for a task) can be read as a Markov blanket, so that the two are plausibly \emph{dual descriptions of one object}. The differences appear to turn on emphasis rather than opposition: the SMN is \emph{constructive} (the architecture is specified first and prediction-error dynamics fall out) where active inference is in part \emph{normative} (agents minimize free energy and the architecture is whatever does so), and the SMN is decentralized, with no single quantity the network optimizes. The one place where the SMN ventures a commitment its neighbour need not share is alert energy: a \emph{real metabolic cost} of the hold, with the load-coupling and post-release dynamics of registers 1 and 2 --- a positive, measurable claim (Section~\ref{sec:discussion}).

\paragraph{Global Workspace Theory.} The broadcast operator $\Pi$ gives a concrete structural mechanism for the kind of shared access Global Workspace Theory describes \citep{Baars2005GlobalWorkspace,Dehaene2014Consciousness}. The two are pitched at different levels --- the SMN at the architecture of coordination, Global Workspace at access --- so the contribution here is to ground "broadcast" in a network operation with measurable consequences (the dual-signal indices of register~8) rather than in a workspace metaphor.

\paragraph{Opponent and equilibrium-point control.} The closest kin in motor control is the equilibrium-point tradition \citep{Feldman1986_EquilibriumPoint,Latash2008EquilibriumPointHistory}: movement as a shift of the equilibrium of antagonist pairs rather than a computed torque. The SMN inherits this and adds the costly, actively maintained hold --- haltability and alert energy --- that the equilibrium-point account does not price.

\paragraph{Conceptual spaces (G\"ardenfors).} As developed in Section~\ref{sec:reafference}, the SMN agent is an embodied realization of the two-vector model, and the body-relative state-space it constructs is, in G\"ardenfors's terms, a conceptual space \citep{Gardenfors2020Events,Gardenfors2019Robot}. We read conceptual spaces as the bridge from \emph{grounded} to \emph{positional} meaning --- the level at which embodied structure becomes a navigable geometry --- a thread we develop in the companion work.

\paragraph{Basal cognition (Levin), an ally.} The SMN's claim that the broadcast is physically realized but medium-independent (Section~\ref{sec:caz}) has a natural realizer in the endogenous bioelectric networks Levin and colleagues describe, where non-neural tissue stores, reads, and acts on shared electrical state \citep{Levin2014MolecularBioelectricity,LevinPezzulo2017EndogenousBioelectricNetworks}. Basal cognition is an ally: cognition without a brain, organized by opponent and field dynamics, is exactly what the SMN's commitments predict should be possible.

\paragraph{Enactivism and registration.} Finally, the SMN is a friend of sensorimotor enactivism, supplying a mechanism (register~4) for perception as the mastery of sensorimotor contingencies \citep{OReganNoe2001Sensorimotor}, and it cashes out Smith's account of objecthood as \emph{registration} through engaged participation \citep{Smith1996OriginObjects} in the dual-signal property of Section~\ref{sec:network}.

\section{Computational tests: the SMN-Lab ledger}\label{sec:ledger}

The architecture's single-zone dynamics are specified; its multi-zone theory is deferred. To exercise the network claims in the interim --- and to put the registers at risk --- we built \textbf{SMN-Lab}, an open simulation bench on the MuJoCo physics engine, organized around a \emph{derived} minimal model organism (the smallest body that can initiate non-inertial movement). Each study is pre-registered with a matched non-modulatory foil (identical except for the one thing the architecture says matters), replicated across seeds; adjustments beyond the published architecture are declared, and confounded runs are discarded rather than reported. Full methods, parameters, and seeded datasets are at \url{https://smn-lab.readthedocs.io}; here we report only the ledger (Table~\ref{tab:ledger}).

The arithmetic is the point: across nine pre-registered studies the architecture was confirmed about as often as it was corrected. The two corrections that bite --- the world model does \emph{not} improve with raw geometry (S1), and self/world resolution \emph{collapses} without modulation (Q1) --- converge on a single sharpened claim, the \emph{resolution principle}: the architecture's explanatory work is done by modulation, not by transduction. An instrument that only ever confirmed its theory would be an illustration; this one is a test.

\section{Discussion}\label{sec:discussion}

\paragraph{What the bench buys, and the gaps it answers.} The multi-zone studies above are working demonstrations of the network the analytic theory leaves open (S0, Q1/Q1b, and the antagonistic-control results all exercise coupling, density, and opponent dynamics across many zones). They also speak to substrate independence: the bench's actuators are simple pull-only opponent motors, not the Hill-type muscle of the reference simulations, so the registers it reproduces already hold in a different physical realization. And the confirm-and-correct ledger is falsification in practice --- the architecture's own predictions were put at risk, and three were corrected.

\paragraph{The decisive measurement still owed.} The architecture's strongest and riskiest claim is that alert energy is a \emph{real metabolic quantity}. The bench can show its functional consequences (Pred-3), but cannot measure biological metabolism. The clean test is in the wet lab: combined EMG and metabolic measurement (NIRS, $^{31}$P-MRS) during sustained isometric tasks, looking for antagonist tonic activation that tracks agonist load linearly (register~1) and decays over seconds to tens of seconds after release rather than vanishing immediately. A positive result would distinguish the SMN from both classical reciprocal inhibition and the bare equilibrium-point hypothesis; a null would falsify its central energetic commitment. We name this as the experiment that matters most and that the bench cannot run.

\paragraph{What a simulation can and cannot carry.} A simulation is not a body and has no phenomenology; nothing here is claimed to feel anything. What a physics engine does carry is the structural and relational conditions the SMN holds to constitute object-directedness --- a body-relative frame, the reafferent separation of self from world, the dual interface, and the halt at resistance. These are what the bench measures. Consistent with this restraint, the world estimate is deflationary: for the living agent there is no stored, contentful world-model, only a current attunement constituted in coupling (Section~\ref{sec:reafference}). Whether the structural signatures are sufficient for experience, or merely necessary, is the question the architecture sharpens, not one it settles.

\paragraph{Forthcoming transitions.} Two threads are deliberately left open. The negotiable-pattern layer (Section~\ref{sec:network}) carries the architecture toward generative structure; its full development --- how negotiable and transactional action patterns ground generative grammar --- is the subject of a companion paper \citep{NagarjunaKarnamNAPprep}. And the passage from the body-grounded snapshot to externalized, serialized meaning --- the grounded-to-positional transition that conceptual spaces mediate --- is the subject of the second monograph in this program. The present paper establishes the basal and haltable layers both build on.

\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

We have proposed an architecture in which cognition is the modulation of sensation through action, built from opponent pairs of elements that sense and act through one substrate, coordinated by a decentralized broadcast, and holding themselves in dynamic equilibrium against the fields they live in. Its organizing claim is that \emph{haltability} --- the active, metabolically costly hold an antagonistic body can recruit --- is the architectural ground of object-directedness: an object is registered as \emph{other} in the moment an unpredicted resistance halts an ongoing action. From this the architecture earns the self/world distinction (reafference), attention (surprise-driven halt), and a route, stated here as a hypothesis, from negotiable action patterns to generative structure. The account keeps what cognitivism and 4E each get right --- generativity and embodiment --- while supplying the mechanism each leaves open, and it commits to eight measurable registers and a confirm-and-correct computational ledger that put it at risk. The single measurement it still owes --- the metabolic correlate of alert energy --- is the experiment we most want done. A theory worth proposing says clearly what would refute it; this is ours.
